Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 May 2026

WFO Tracker uses your Google account only to access and update your calendar data required for the app features.

Information We Access

  • Google Calendar metadata and events needed to create and update your private WFO Tracker calendar.
  • Basic OAuth session data (access tokens) in your browser local storage to keep you signed in temporarily.

How We Use Information

  • Display your monthly office/home/leave/workcation status.
  • Create and maintain a private calendar named WFO Tracker in your Google Calendar account.
  • Compute office attendance statistics and projections.

Data Storage

  • This app stores session and app preferences locally in your browser (localStorage).
  • Calendar event data is stored in your own Google Calendar account.
  • No separate backend database is used by this app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data is processed only through Google APIs to provide app functionality.

Security

OAuth access is handled through Google Sign-In. You can revoke app access anytime from your Google Account permissions page.

Contact

For privacy questions, please open an issue at https://github.com/gshreya10/wfo-tracker/issues.
